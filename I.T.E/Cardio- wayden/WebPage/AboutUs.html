    Eureka is a new start up founded in December 2016. Our mission statement is "Expanding our students’ horizons by providing a unique learning and cultural experience. " We aim to deliver a unique learning experience. That is why, rather than the traditional memorization approach to language education, we provide a complete cultural and language immersion program to make learning a fun and enriching experience.
