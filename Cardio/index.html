About

What is Eureka ?

Eureka is a language academy offering all students and adults the oportunity to learn spanish in the most interactive and effective way: Through immersion. In our academy and online classes and activities, our students will improve their fluency in Spanish by interacting with native speakers and getting involved in enriching cultural activities in the language. One may sign up using our online registration to choose a plan of study which may include private tutoring, group face-to-face /online language and culture classes, CSEC and CAPE exam preparation, scheduled conversations with native speakers via skype and access to our extense online database of notes and other reading and listening material.

About

Why choose Eureka?

Lack of time and resources often hinder the learning process of children in public schools. For students who wish to pursue a career in languages or who simply want to expand their horizons, Eureka provides language and cultural immersion without having to travel abroad or even leaving the comfort of your home. Children and young adults will be able to immerse themselves in the Spanish language at a relatively low price, compared to the language immersion programmes offered by schools. You never know: your child might own the next prosperous business in the region and need Spanish to talk to his/her future most promising investors! Imagine what that could mean for you and for the unity and development of the Caribbean region. Join us now! You won't regret it!

Services

Achieve the best results with our variety of learning activities!

Preparation for external examinations

Our classes and cultural activities are carefully selected to help students master their reading, writing and speaking skills in the spanish language and achieve excellent results in their CSEC and CAPE examinations

Online sessions and videoconferencing

Subscribe to enjoy live or recorded interactive sessions with our professional tutors. You can also practice your conversation skills with our native speakers via videoconferencing.In them, you may learn about trending topics while improving your fluency in Spanish.

Cultural Immerssion

Meet our multinational staff and participate in fun cultural activities, ranging from story-telling, folk-song singing, and dancing, to watching spanish films and performing traditional plays. Get involved and start learning about the cultural diversity of spanish-speaking territories!
